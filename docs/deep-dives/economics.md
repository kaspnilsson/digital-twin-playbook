# Economics

## The SaaS Stack

An AI power user typically pays:

- ChatGPT Plus: $20/month
- Claude Pro: $20/month
- Cursor Pro: $20/month

**Total: $60/month** for three siloed ecosystems.

## The Digital Twin Stack

- **VPS:** $6/month (DigitalOcean or similar).
- **Supabase:** $0 (Free tier).
- **TypingMind:** $40 (One-time license).
- **API Compute:** Variable. Average usage (~50M tokens) costs ~$36 via OpenRouter -- depends on your model usage. Claude Opus is *very* expensive.

**Monthly cost: $42.**

## The Tradeoff

Metered API costs often exceed a flat $20 subscription for heavy users. This is intentional.

Subsidized $20 subscriptions allow vendors to lock users into proprietary ecosystems. OpenAI and Anthropic burn billions to acquire data and users, leaving you with no leverage when they change terms or degrade models. 

Metered compute reflects real costs. You can optimize usage, cache requests, and switch providers without losing history.

## The Sovereignty Premium

You pay to own your database. This premium grants:

- Portability between AI providers.
- Absolute control over personal context.
- Freedom to migrate without starting over.

The cost of ownership is negligible compared to the risk of vendor lock-in.
